!! WHAT IS THE BUSINESS ABOUT !!

Situated in a row of 100 year old but newly renovated railway sheds in the charming and popular hinterland town of Palmwoods,
Chew Chew Bistro offers a relaxed dining experience in a truly unique environment by the tracks.
With a chic vintage decor and plush chesterfield lounges, our licensed bistro offers a delicious all day breakfast and lunch menu,
a decadent range of house made cakes and pastries as well as our fabulous coffee and of course we are licensed so you can enjoy a drink (or two)
while you enjoy the unique ambience of dining by the tracks.

Chew Chew Bistro is conveniantly located next to the The Shed, an emporium which offers new and secondhand furniture,
unique interior pieces, antiques and collectables, artwork, jewellery and much more.
