The Shed offers a carefully curated selection of high-quality new and second-hand designer furniture, interiors, art, décor, and jewelry sourced from all over the world.

The Shed is a family-owned boutique that specializes in designer new and second-hand furniture and interiors. Our exquisite collection of home décor pieces, art, and beautiful jewelry has been carefully curated to cater to the refined taste of our clientele. At the heart of the The Shed are the stores curators, both of whom possess a background in property and interior design, and are dedicated to providing a unique and inspiring shopping experience; "We pride ourselves on stocking only the most beautiful and interesting items. Everything must be either beautiful or interesting, of high quality and condition, or collectable and quirky!".

Our selection includes vintage furniture that has been restored to its original glory, as well as new and vintage homewares, art, and décor pieces. We understand the importance of sustainability, and that's why we emphasize the use of vintage furniture in our collection. We believe that by offering a more eco-friendly option, we are contributing to a healthier environment. Our inventory is sourced from all over the world, including Europe, the Americas, Asia, and Southeast Asia. We also welcome consignment stock. At The Shed, we offer a shopping experience that is both sophisticated and enchanting.


We invite you to visit us and immerse yourself in the special energy and charm that we know you will love.
