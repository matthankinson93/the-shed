Terms and Conditions


  • For functions, our private dining room is available for groups of 8 or more guests.
  • For groups of over 15, we offer a bespoke functions menu in place of our standard restaurant menu.
  • For groups larger than 8, we kindly request a deposit of $5 per head to secure your booking.
  • To ensure a seamless dining experience, we require that all bills are settled in one transaction.
  • Regrettably, we are unable to accommodate split bills at this time.
  • Cancellations must be made no later than 1 week prior to the booking date to qualify for a full refund of your deposit. Bookings cancelled after this time will be subject to forfeiture of the deposit.

We reserve the right to decline service or cancel a reservation at our discretion. In the event that any adjustments need to be made to your booking, we ask that you please notify us as soon as possible.

We extend our sincerest gratitude for your understanding and cooperation with these guidelines, as they are essential to providing the exceptional dining experience we strive to deliver.
